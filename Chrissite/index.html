Biography

Christine Weston Chandler (often known online as Chris Chan) is an American internet personality who became widely known in the mid-2000s for creating the webcomic series Sonichu. Christine gained online attention through personal videos, artwork, and frequent interaction with early internet communities.

Much of the attention surrounding Christine has come from online communities discussing her comics, videos, and personal updates. She has been a significant figure in early internet culture, particularly in discussions about online identity and parasocial attention.
