Biography

Christine Weston Chandler (often known online as Chris Chan) is an American creator recognized primarily for her webcomic series Sonichu. Christine first gained attention through her artwork, online videos, and active participation in internet communities during the early 2000s.

Christine’s work, especially Sonichu, became widely circulated within various online communities. Over time, she became a frequently discussed figure in early internet culture, partially due to the visibility of her creative output and personal online interactions.

About Sonichu

Sonichu is Christine’s long-running webcomic series blending elements of Sonic and Pokémon–styled characters. The story follows Sonichu and Rosechu, who adventure through colorful, cartoon-like worlds influenced by video game themes.

Although the series has a unique style that gained attention online, it also serves as a personal creative outlet for Christine. Many people have followed the series over the years due to its distinctive artwork and creative storytelling.
